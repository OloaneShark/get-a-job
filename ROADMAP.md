
# Get-A-Job Roadmap

Get-A-Job is being built as a complete AI-powered Job Search Command Center.

The goal is to help users not only track applications, but also discover jobs, research companies, optimize resumes, prepare for interviews, and automate large portions of the job search process.

# Vision

The goal is not to build another job tracker.

The goal is to build the operating system for finding a better career.

Everything—from discovering opportunities, researching companies, optimizing resumes, generating application materials, preparing for interviews, tracking progress, and leveraging AI—should happen inside a single platform.
